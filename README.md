# Raw Ore Pellets
https://modrinth.com/project/raw-ore-pellets

This mod adds three new items: **Raw Copper Pellet**, **Raw Iron Pellet**, and **Raw Gold Pellet**. These items function as the equivalent of nuggets for raw ores.

![Raw Ore Pellets](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/e3597ec50d76a09e156721b1245050ac4bb9f6a1.png)

## Mining changes:

**Ore blocks no longer always drop raw ores** - The drops depend on the tool used:
- Lower-tier tools (e.g. stone pickaxe) drop a small number of pellets
- Higher-tier tools increase the amount of pellets dropped
- Diamond pickaxes and above drop raw iron and raw gold as in vanilla
- Copper pickaxes and above drop one raw copper.

Copper is limited to a maximum of one raw copper per ore block (same rule as iron or gold).

## Smelting:

- Pellets can be smelted into their corresponding nuggets
- Raw ore blocks can be smelted directly into their corresponding metal blocks
- Smelting times are adjusted to remain consistent with vanilla balance

## Purpose:

- Gives a purpose to copper tools by making iron rarer only during the early game
- Makes early-game mining less efficient than mid/late-game
- Preserves vanilla behavior for high-tier tools
- Reduces the amount of copper obtained from mining to a reasonable value
